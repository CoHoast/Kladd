Frequently asked questions

What's included in the free trial?
You get full access to all features of your selected plan for 14 days. No credit card required to start. Connect your integrations, generate policies, and see your compliance score — all during the trial.
Can I switch plans later?
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When you upgrade, you'll get immediate access to new features. When you downgrade, changes take effect at the end of your billing cycle.
Do you offer discounts for startups?
Yes, we offer a 50% discount for early-stage startups (under $1M in funding or revenue). Contact us at startups@kladd.io to apply.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and can arrange ACH or wire transfers for annual Enterprise plans.
Is there a setup fee?
No setup fees. You can get started immediately after signing up. For Enterprise customers, we offer optional onboarding assistance at no additional cost.
What happens to my data if I cancel?
You can export all your data at any time. After cancellation, we retain your data for 30 days in case you change your mind, then securely delete it.
